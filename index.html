Who we are

What do a seasoned student entrepreneur and an inexperienced idea-holder have in common?

They both will fit into the UMass Entrepreneurship Club perfectly.

Why us

Catering to many skill-levels, the club is developing spaces for both first-timers and 'old'-timers to learn, problem solve, and own their life.

Networking

Networking is an extremely crucial aspect for success in all regards. At UMass Entrepreneurship Club, you will be able to talk with individuals from all walks of life and listen to their experiences, successes, and failures. Networking at our club isnâ€™t just about meeting new people and collecting new contacts, but about creating meaningful relationships and improving and developing yourself in the process.

Guest Speakers

Learning from one another is important in cultivating a successful entrepreneurial mindset, but having the opportunity to listen, learn and speak with professionals in the field provides a large advantage and creates opportunities for advancing your self success. Here at the UMass Entrepreneurship Club, we have a focus on bringing in a variety of seasoned entrepreneurs to talk about their ventures and experiences.

Startup Events

We host various events such as ULaunch, where you will be able to form a team, build and pitch a business model, and meet some ambitious individuals just like you. In addition, we will have speakers, workshops, mentors, and judges to assist you along the process. There is absolutely no experience necessary, just a craving to Own Your Life
